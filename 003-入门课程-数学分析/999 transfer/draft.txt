\cos3\alpha\\

=\cos(\alpha+2\alpha)\\

=\cos\alpha\cdot\cos2\alpha
-\sin\alpha\cdot\sin2\alpha\\

=\cos\alpha\cdot(2\cos^2\alpha-1)
-2\sin\alpha\cdot\sin\alpha\cdot\cos\alpha\\

=2\cos^3\alpha-\cos\alpha
-2(1-\cos^2\alpha)\cdot\cos\alpha\\

=2\cos^3\alpha-\cos\alpha
-2\cos\alpha+2\cos^3\alpha\\

=4\cos^3\alpha-3\cos\alpha
