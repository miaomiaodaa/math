**内容：**  
设  $H=\begin{bmatrix}  
A_{m\times m}&C_{m\times n}\\\   
O_{n\times m}&B_{n\times n}  
\end{bmatrix}$  是分块矩阵，若 $A，B$  均可逆，  
  
则 $H$ 可逆，且 $H^{-1}=\begin{bmatrix}  
A^{-1}&-A^{-1}CB^{-1}\\\   
O&B^{-1}\end{bmatrix}$   
  
**举例**  
 $\left[\begin{array}{cccc:cc}  
1&2&3&4&5&6\\\   
6&5&4&3&2&1\\\   
7&8&9&4&5&6\\\   
6&5&4&7&8&9\\\   
\hdashline  
1&5&9&3&5&7\\\   
2&5&8&4&5&6  
\end{array}\right]$
