 $((A^\star)^\star)^\star  
=|A^\star|^{n-2}\cdot|A^\star|  
=|A|^{(n-1)(n-2)}\cdot|A|\cdot A^{-1}$

 $(A^\star)^\star=|A|^{n-2}\cdot A$   
  
设 $A=A^\star$

=|A^\star|^{n-2}\cdot A^\star$
