**例1**  
 $k=1$  
 $D=\begin{vmatrix}  
1&1&2&3\\\  
0&0&8&9\\\  
2&5&2&4\\\  
9&9&9&10  
\end{vmatrix}$  
  
 $=9\times A_{11}+9\times A_{11}+9\times A_{13}  
+10\times A_{14}$  
  
 $=D_1=\begin{vmatrix}  
9&9&9&10\\\  
0&0&8&9\\\  
2&5&2&4\\\  
9&9&9&10  
\end{vmatrix}=0$

1&1&0&-5\\\  
-1&3&1&3\\\  
2&-4&-1&-3  
\end{vmatrix}$  
  
 $A_{11}+A_{12}+A_{13}+A_{14}  
=\begin{vmatrix}  
1&1&1&1\\\  
1&1&0&-5\\\  
-1&3&1&3\\\  
2&-4&-1&-3  
\end{vmatrix}$  
  
 $M_{11}+M_{21}+M_{31}+M_{41}$  
  
 $=A_{11}-A_{21}+A_{31}-A_{41}  
=\begin{vmatrix}  
1&-1&1&-1\\\  
1&1&0&-5\\\  
-1&3&1&3\\\  
2&-4&-1&-3  
\end{vmatrix}$  
  
 $2A_{11}-4A_{12}-A_{13}-3A_{14}  
=\begin{vmatrix}  
2&-4&-1&-3\\\  
1&1&0&-5\\\  
-1&3&1&3\\\  
2&-4&-1&-3  
\end{vmatrix}=0$
